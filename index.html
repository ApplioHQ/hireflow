Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is Applio really free to use?

Yes. The Free plan gives you the full Resume Builder, all 9 templates, Job Tracker, and 10 PDF downloads — no credit card required. AI features and unlimited downloads are part of Premium or Lifetime.

How does the AI tailor my resume?

You paste a job description and our AI rewrites your bullets, optimizes keywords, and rewrites your summary to match what the role is asking for — grounded in your actual experience, never inventing things.

What is ATS and why does it matter?

An Applicant Tracking System filters resumes before a human ever sees them. Around 70% of resumes never make it past this stage. Applio scores your resume against the ATS rubric and tells you exactly what to fix.

What templates are available?

9 professionally designed templates: Modern, Classic, Creative, Minimal, Professional, Tech, Executive, Compact, and Elegant. Every template is fully customizable — change colors, fonts, spacing, and toggle sections on or off.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account menu inside the app — no questions, no friction. You keep Premium access until the end of your billing period.

Is my data secure?

Passwords are hashed with PBKDF2-SHA256 (100k iterations), never stored in plain text. Payment data is handled by Stripe directly — Applio never touches your card. Your resume data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
