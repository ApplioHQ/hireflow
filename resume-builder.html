Free Resume Builder

Updated 2026-07-10 · Applio
Applio is a free online resume builder that helps you create an ATS-ready resume in minutes. Pick a recruiter-tested template, import your existing resume or type it in, and export a clean PDF — no credit card and no watermark on the free plan.

How the resume builder works

  1. Start from a template or import. Choose one of 16 ATS-friendly designs, or upload an existing PDF/DOCX and let Applio parse it into editable sections.
  2. Fill in your sections. Add your experience, education, skills, and projects. A live preview shows exactly what recruiters and the PDF will see.
  3. Optimize and export. Check your ATS score against a job, tighten your bullets, then download a clean PDF (or HTML/TXT) ready to submit.

What you get for free

  • All 16 professional, fully customizable templates (fonts, colors, spacing, section order).
  • AI resume import from PDF, DOCX, or TXT so you don't retype anything.
  • A built-in job-application tracker and AI interview prep.
  • A live preview that matches the exported PDF exactly — no surprises.
  • Clean, single-column, ATS-safe formatting by default.

Frequently asked questions

Is the resume builder really free?

Yes. You can build a resume, use all 16 templates, import an existing resume, and download 10 PDFs per month for free — no credit card required. Premium unlocks unlimited downloads and the AI writing tools.

Are the resumes ATS-friendly?

Yes. Every template uses a single-column, standard-heading layout with real selectable text, which is what applicant tracking systems parse most reliably. You can also score your resume against a job with the built-in ATS checker.

Can I import my existing resume?

Yes. Upload a PDF, DOCX, or TXT and Applio extracts your details into editable sections, so you don't have to retype anything.

Will there be a watermark on my resume?

No. Exported resumes have no Applio watermark on any plan.
