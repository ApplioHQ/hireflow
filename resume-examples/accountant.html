Accountant resume bullet point examples

  • Managed the full month-end close for accounts totaling $X, reducing close time by X days.
  • Reconciled general ledger accounts monthly, identifying and correcting discrepancies to ensure accuracy.
  • Prepared financial statements in accordance with GAAP for review by leadership and auditors.
  • Processed accounts payable and receivable, improving on-time payment rates and reducing aging balances.
  • Streamlined a reconciliation process, cutting manual work by X hours per month.
  • Supported annual audits by preparing schedules and documentation, contributing to clean audit results.
  • Assisted with budgeting and forecasting, improving variance-analysis accuracy for department heads.
  • Implemented internal-control improvements that strengthened compliance and reduced errors.

Frequently asked questions

What should an accountant emphasize on a resume?

Emphasize accuracy, compliance, and efficiency: GAAP knowledge, reconciliations, timely close, and any process improvements that saved time or money. Note the size of the accounts or ledgers you manage to convey scope.

Do I need a CPA on my accounting resume?

A CPA isn't required for every role but is highly valued and required for some. If you hold or are pursuing it, list it clearly; also mention relevant software and any specialized areas like tax or audit.

What software should I list?

List the ERP and accounting tools you've used, such as QuickBooks, SAP, Oracle, or NetSuite, plus advanced Excel. Matching the software named in the job posting helps you pass applicant screening.
