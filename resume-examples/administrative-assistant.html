Frequently asked questions

What skills matter most on an Administrative Assistant resume?

Organization, communication, and software proficiency. Highlight calendar and travel management, Microsoft Office, and your ability to juggle competing priorities reliably and discreetly.

How do I quantify administrative work?

Use numbers like the number of executives or team members supported, calls or emails handled daily, hours saved through process improvements, and budgets or expenses managed.

Should I tailor my resume to the office software listed in the job?

Yes. Match the tools named in the posting, such as Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, or scheduling software, since these are often screening keywords for administrative roles.
