Business Analyst resume bullet point examples

  • Gathered and documented requirements from X+ business stakeholders across finance and operations, translating needs into user stories that reduced rework during development by X%.
  • Analyzed process data using SQL and Excel to identify a bottleneck, then recommended a workflow change that cut cycle time by X%.
  • Facilitated X requirements workshops per quarter, aligning product, engineering, and business teams on scope and priorities.
  • Built Power BI dashboards tracking X KPIs, giving leadership real-time visibility that shortened monthly reporting from X days to X hours.
  • Mapped current- and future-state processes using BPMN, uncovering X redundant steps and streamlining a core approval workflow.
  • Coordinated user acceptance testing across X test cases, resolving defects before release and improving on-time delivery.
  • Authored functional specifications and acceptance criteria that reduced ambiguous requirements and lowered change requests by X%.
  • Partnered with vendors to evaluate X software options, delivering a cost-benefit analysis that informed a $X purchasing decision.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need SQL on a Business Analyst resume?

SQL is one of the most commonly requested skills for BA roles because it lets you pull and analyze data independently. If you know it, list it prominently and show it in a bullet; if not, highlight Excel and BI tools you do use.

Should a Business Analyst resume be technical or business-focused?

Both. Show that you understand business goals and stakeholders while demonstrating the technical fluency (data analysis, process modeling, tools) to turn those goals into actionable requirements.

What metrics work best on a BA resume?

Focus on efficiency and decision impact: cycle-time reductions, hours saved, defect rates, adoption rates, and cost savings tied to your analysis or requirements work.
