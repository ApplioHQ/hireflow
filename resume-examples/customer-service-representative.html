Frequently asked questions

What metrics should a customer service resume include?

Include metrics like CSAT, first-contact resolution rate, average handle time, ticket or call volume, and quality-assurance scores. These numbers show employers you deliver results in high-volume environments.

What skills matter most for customer service roles?

Communication, empathy, problem-solving, and de-escalation top the list, alongside familiarity with CRM and helpdesk tools like Salesforce or Zendesk. Balance soft skills with the specific systems and channels you've supported.

How do I stand out with limited experience?

Highlight transferable skills from any role involving people, sales, or problem-solving, and quantify volume and satisfaction wherever possible. A brief summary emphasizing reliability and communication helps entry-level candidates stand out.
