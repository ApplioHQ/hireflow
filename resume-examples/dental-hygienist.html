Frequently asked questions

Where should I put my license on a dental hygienist resume?

Near the top, in your header or a dedicated credentials line. List your RDH license, state, and expiration along with CPR/BLS certification, since practices verify these first.

Do local anesthesia or laser certifications help?

Yes, if you hold them and your state permits those procedures. Listing expanded-function or local anesthesia certification signals a broader scope and can make you more competitive.

How do I stand out beyond clinical duties?

Highlight patient education outcomes, treatment plan acceptance, efficiency with a full schedule, and any experience mentoring or onboarding. These show you add value beyond routine cleanings.
