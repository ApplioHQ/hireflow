Frequently asked questions

Should I list my license on an electrician resume?

Yes, prominently. State your license level (apprentice, journeyman, or master) and the state, since employers screen for it first. Include any active certifications or your union affiliation if applicable.

What certifications matter for electricians?

State licensure is primary. OSHA 10 or 30 safety cards, and specialty credentials such as low-voltage or specific manufacturer certifications, add value. Only list credentials you actually hold.

How do I show experience without an office-style resume?

Quantify project scope: amperage, square footage, project value, inspection pass rates, and safety record. These concrete numbers demonstrate skill and reliability to hiring contractors.
