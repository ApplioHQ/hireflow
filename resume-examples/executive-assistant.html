Executive Assistant Resume Examples & Template

Updated 2026-07-08 · Applio
A strong executive assistant resume shows you keep senior leaders and their operations running smoothly, with proof of discretion, organization, and initiative. Quantify the scope you support such as number of executives, meetings, or budget managed.

Executive Assistant resume bullet point examples

  • Managed complex, shifting calendars for X executives, resolving conflicts and protecting time for priorities.
  • Coordinated domestic and international travel and itineraries, reducing booking costs by X% through preferred vendors.
  • Prepared and reconciled expense reports totaling $X annually with full accuracy and on-time submission.
  • Organized X board and leadership meetings, preparing agendas, materials, and minutes.
  • Served as a trusted gatekeeper, screening communications and handling confidential information with discretion.
  • Planned company events and offsites for up to X attendees, managing logistics and budgets end to end.
  • Streamlined scheduling and document workflows, saving executives an estimated X hours per week.
  • Coordinated cross-functional projects, tracking deadlines and following up to keep deliverables on schedule.

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between an executive assistant and an administrative assistant on a resume?

Executive assistants support senior leaders and handle higher-stakes, confidential work like board support and strategic coordination. Emphasize the seniority of who you supported and the complexity of your responsibilities.

How do I show trustworthiness on paper?

Highlight experience handling confidential information, supporting C-suite leaders, and managing sensitive materials such as board documents or financials. Discretion and reliability are core to the role.

Which software should I list?

Include the tools you use daily: Microsoft Office or Google Workspace, plus scheduling, expense, and communication platforms like Concur or Slack. Match the stack in the job posting where you can.
