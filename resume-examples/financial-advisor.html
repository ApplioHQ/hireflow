Frequently asked questions

Which licenses should a financial advisor list?

List active registrations such as Series 7 and Series 66 (or 63 and 65), plus designations like CFP or ChFC if you hold them. These are often screening requirements, so place them near the top.

How do I quantify success without breaking client confidentiality?

Use aggregate figures like total assets under management, client retention rate, and percentage growth in your book. These show results without disclosing individual client details.

Should I mention my sales numbers?

Framing matters. Emphasize client outcomes, relationship growth, and retention alongside production figures so you read as a trusted advisor rather than purely a salesperson.
