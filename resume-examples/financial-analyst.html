Frequently asked questions

How important is Excel on a Financial Analyst resume?

Very. Advanced Excel is a core requirement, so demonstrate it with specifics like financial modeling, pivot tables, and lookups, and show automation or analysis you built rather than just listing the skill.

Should I mention certifications like the CFA?

Yes, if you hold or are pursuing the CFA, CPA, or FMVA, list it, since these are recognized in finance. Indicate the level or candidacy status accurately rather than overstating it.

What metrics belong on a Financial Analyst resume?

Highlight forecast accuracy, cost savings identified, budget size managed, reporting time reduced, and the dollar value of decisions your analysis supported.
