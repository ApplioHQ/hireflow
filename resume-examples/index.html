Software Engineer Resume Examples & Template
A strong software engineer resume leads with quantified impact (performance, scale, reliability), names the specific languages and systems you used, and shows shipped work rather than listing responsibilities.
Project Manager Resume Examples & Template
A strong project manager resume proves you deliver projects on time and on budget by quantifying scope, budget, team size, and outcomes.
Registered Nurse Resume Examples & Template
A strong registered nurse resume prominently lists your active RN license, certifications (BLS, ACLS), and clinical specialty, then backs it up with patient-care outcomes and volumes.
Data Analyst Resume Examples & Template
A strong data analyst resume pairs technical tools (SQL, Excel, Python, and a BI tool like Tableau or Power BI) with the business decisions your analysis drove.
Marketing Manager Resume Examples & Template
A strong marketing manager resume quantifies campaign results (leads, revenue, ROI, growth) and shows leadership across channels, budget, and teams.
Teacher Resume Examples & Template
A strong teacher resume highlights your state certification, grade level and subject expertise, and classroom outcomes like improved test scores or engagement.
Accountant Resume Examples & Template
A strong accountant resume emphasizes accuracy, compliance, and process improvements, backed by knowledge of GAAP and accounting software.
Customer Service Representative Resume Examples & Template
A strong customer service representative resume showcases communication skills and measurable service metrics like satisfaction (CSAT) scores, resolution rates, and call volume.
Business Analyst Resume Examples & Template
A strong Business Analyst resume pairs requirements-gathering and data analysis with clear business outcomes.
Sales Representative Resume Examples & Template
A standout Sales Representative resume leads with numbers: quota attainment, revenue generated, and deals closed.
Graphic Designer Resume Examples & Template
A strong Graphic Designer resume balances creative skill with business impact.

A strong HR Manager resume balances people leadership with measurable business impact.

Financial Analyst Resume Examples & Template
A strong Financial Analyst resume pairs technical modeling and analysis skills with measurable business impact.
Operations Manager Resume Examples & Template
A strong Operations Manager resume emphasizes efficiency, team leadership, and cost control.
Administrative Assistant Resume Examples & Template
A strong Administrative Assistant resume showcases organization, communication, and reliability.
Product Manager Resume Examples & Template
A strong product manager resume leads with business and user outcomes you drove, not features you shipped.
UX Designer Resume Examples & Template
A strong UX designer resume shows your end-to-end process, from research to validated design, and connects your work to usability and business metrics.
Web Developer Resume Examples & Template
A strong web developer resume pairs a clear tech stack with bullets that show what you built and the measurable result, such as faster load times or higher conversion.
Electrician Resume Examples & Template
A strong electrician resume leads with your license level and clearly lists code knowledge, safety record, and the systems you have worked on.
Financial Advisor Resume Examples & Template

Dental Hygienist Resume Examples & Template
