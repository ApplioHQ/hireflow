Marketing Manager Resume Examples & Template

Updated 2026-07-08 · Applio
A strong marketing manager resume quantifies campaign results (leads, revenue, ROI, growth) and shows leadership across channels, budget, and teams. Lead with measurable wins and name the channels and tools you own, from SEO and paid media to marketing automation.

Frequently asked questions

What should a marketing manager emphasize on a resume?

Emphasize measurable results: leads, revenue, ROI, traffic, and growth percentages tied to campaigns you led. Also show the channels you own and the size of budgets and teams you've managed.

Which metrics matter most for marketing resumes?

Metrics like ROI/ROAS, cost per acquisition, conversion rate, lead volume, pipeline influenced, and traffic growth resonate most. Frame them as example ranges if you can't share exact figures publicly.

Should I tailor my resume to the channel?

Yes. If a role is paid-media heavy, foreground PPC and analytics; if it's brand or content focused, lead with those wins. Mirror the language in the job description to pass screening.
