Frequently asked questions

What should a project manager emphasize on a resume?

Emphasize outcomes tied to the classic project constraints: scope, time, budget, and quality. Quantify team size, budget managed, and on-time delivery rates so hiring managers can gauge the scale you operate at.

Do I need a PMP to be a project manager?

A PMP isn't always required, but certifications like PMP, CAPM, or CSM strengthen your resume and are prized in many industries. If you hold one, list it prominently near your name or in a certifications section.

What's a common mistake on PM resumes?

Listing responsibilities instead of results is the most common mistake. Instead of "responsible for managing projects," show the number of projects, their value, and the measurable outcome you delivered.
