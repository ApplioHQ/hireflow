Frequently asked questions

Do I need a portfolio link on my UX resume?

Yes. A portfolio is essential for UX roles because hiring managers want to see your process and craft. Place the link prominently in your header, and make sure the case studies show your reasoning, not just final screens.

Should I list UI skills too?

If you do both, yes. Many roles blend UX and UI, so listing tools like Figma alongside visual and interaction design signals versatility. Just make sure your research and problem-solving still lead.

How technical should a UX resume be?

Show enough to collaborate with engineers, such as understanding of responsive design or accessibility standards. Deep coding skill is optional unless the role is explicitly UX engineering.
